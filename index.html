project websites

Brain Diffusion atlas of the Neonate Brain

Interactive atlas of diffusion-weighted mri derived metrics. I developed this to make outcomes of a study at Yale School of Medicine publicly available - we aimed to provide a normative atlas for diffusivity metrics, which could ultimately lead to better diagnosis of unilateral ischemic hypoxic brain damage in neonates.


Breast Implant Volume Calculator

Application to calculate implant volume based on CT-derived measurements - I developed this website for a team from reconstructive surgery at Yale during Breast Cancer Awareness Month to facilitate breast reconstruction

research

Currently working on my MD thesis about Autism Spectrum Disorder-related connectome alterations.
I investigate the relation between geodesic distance and resting state functional connectivity in ASD during a research internship at the Montreal Neurological Institute/McGill Univeristy, Canada.
During my time as research scholar at the Yale School of Medicine, Department of Neuroradiology, I studied alterations in microstructure based on diffusion weighted MR images. This study was presented at the Annual Meeting of the American Society of Functional Neuroradiology (Sep. 21, Santa Fe, NM) and at the Annual Meeting of the Radiological Society of North America (Nov. 21, Chicago, IL), upcoming poster presentation at the Annual Meetin of the American Society for Pediatric Neuroradiology (ASPNR, Jan. 22, Miami, FL).
I am part of the Luebeck-based Social Neuroscience Lab.
